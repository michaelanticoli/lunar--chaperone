Throat Sounding

Vagus Nerve Activation · Toning & Resonance

Sustained vocal toning channeled through the throat engages the vagus nerve, shifting autonomic state from sympathetic dominance into a resonant parasympathetic field. The sound becomes a carrier wave for somatic reorganization.

Protocol III

The Descent Stretch

Structural Descent · Hip Flexor & Sacral Release

A bilateral hip-flexor and sacral-release sequence that completes the somatic circuit initiated in Crown Breathing. Energy raised through the crown is grounded through deliberate structural descent into the pelvic bowl and lower extremities.

check_circle

Mandatory

  • — Empty bladder before beginning
  • — Warm the hip joints (5 min walking)
  • — Use a folded blanket under knees
  • — Hold each position ≥ 90 seconds
warning

Caution

  • — Contraindicated post-lumbar surgery
  • — Do not force pelvic floor release
  • — Discontinue if sharp sacral pain
  • — Avoid in late pregnancy

Begin prone with hands beneath the shoulders. On each exhale, allow gravity to soften the hip flexors. Surrender is the operative principle — do not force depth, allow the structural intelligence of the body to find its own floor.
